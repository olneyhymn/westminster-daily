## Lord's Day 36

### The Third Commandment (1)

*Christian Gratitude*

#### Question 99

What is required in the third commandment?

##### Answer

That we, not only by cursing [^fna] or perjury, [^fnb] but also by rash swearing, [^fnc] must not profane or abuse the name of God; nor by silence or connivance be partakers of these horrible sins in others; [^fnd] and, briefly, that we use the holy name of God no otherwise than with fear and reverence; [^fne] so that he may be rightly confessed [^fnf] and worshipped by us, [^fng] and be glorified in all our words and works. [^fnh]

#### Question 100

Is then the profaning of God's name, by swearing and cursing, so heinous a sin, that his wrath is kindled against those who do not endeavour, as much as in them lies, to prevent and forbid such cursing and swearing?

##### Answer

It undoubtedly is, [^fna] for there is no sin greater or more provoking to God, than the profaning of his name; and therefore he has commanded this sin to be punished with death. [^fnb]
