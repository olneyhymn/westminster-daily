## Lord's Day 48

### The Second Petition

*Christian Gratitude*

#### Question 123

Which is the second petition?

##### Answer

"Thy kingdom come"; that is, rule us so by thy word and Spirit, that we may submit ourselves more and more to thee; [^fna] preserve and increase thy church; [^fnb] destroy the works of the devil, and all violence which would exalt itself against thee; and also all wicked counsels devised against thy holy word; [^fnc] till the full perfection of thy kingdom take place, [^fnd] wherein thou shalt be all in all. [^fne]
