## Lord's Day 1

### Our Only Comfort

*Introduction*

#### Question 1

What is thy only comfort in life and death?

##### Answer

That I with body and soul, both in life and death, [^fn1a] am not my own, [^fn1b] but belong unto my faithful Saviour Jesus Christ; [^fn1c] who, with his precious blood, has fully satisfied for all my sins, [^fn1d] and delivered me from all the power of the devil; [^fn1e] and so preserves me [^fn1f] that without the will of my heavenly Father, not a hair can fall from my head; [^fn1g] yea, that all things must be subservient to my salvation, [^fn1h] and therefore, by his Holy Spirit, He also assures me of eternal life, [^fn1i] and makes me sincerely willing and ready, henceforth, to live unto him. [^fn1j]

#### Question 2

How many things are necessary for thee to know, that thou, enjoying this comfort, mayest live and die happily?

##### Answer

Three; [^fn2a] the first, how great my sins and miseries are; [^fn2b] the second, how I may be delivered from all my sins and miseries; [^fn2c] the third, how I shall express my gratitude to God for such deliverance. [^fn2d]
