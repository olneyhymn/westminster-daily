## Lord's Day 24

### Good Works

*Divine Deliverance*

#### Question 62

But why cannot our good works be the whole, or part of our righteousness before God?

##### Answer

Because, that the righteousness, which can be approved of before the tribunal of God, must be absolutely perfect, [^fna] and in all respects conformable to the divine law; and also, that our best works in this life are all imperfect and defiled with sin. [^fnb]

#### Question 63

What! do not our good works merit, which yet God will reward in this and in a future life?

##### Answer

This reward is not of merit, but of grace. [^fna]

#### Question 64

But does not this doctrine make men careless and profane?

##### Answer

By no means: for it is impossible that those, who are implanted into Christ by a true faith, should not bring forth fruits of thankfulness. [^fna]
