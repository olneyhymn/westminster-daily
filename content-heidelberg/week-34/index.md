## Lord's Day 34

### The Law of God and the First Commandment

*Christian Gratitude*

#### Question 92

What is the law of God?

##### Answer

Question 93: How are these commandments divided?

#### Question 93

How are these commandments divided?

##### Answer

Into two tables; [^fn93a] the first of which teaches us how we must behave towards God; the second, what duties we owe to our neighbour. [^fn93b]

#### Question 94

What does God enjoin in the first commandment?

##### Answer

That I, as sincerely as I desire the salvation of my own soul, avoid and flee from all idolatry, [^fn94a] sorcery, soothsaying, superstition, [^fn94b] invocation of saints, or any other creatures; [^fn94c] and learn rightly to know the only true God; [^fn94d] trust in him alone, [^fn94e] with humility [^fn94f] and patience submit to him; [^fn94g] expect all good things from him only; [^fn94h] love, [^fn94i] fear, [^fn94j] and glorify him with my whole heart; [^fn94k] so that I renounce and forsake all creatures, rather than commit even the least thing contrary to his will. [^fn94l]

#### Question 95

What is idolatry?

##### Answer

Idolatry is, instead of, or besides that one true God, who has manifested himself in his word, to contrive, or have any other object, in which men place their trust. [^fn95a]
