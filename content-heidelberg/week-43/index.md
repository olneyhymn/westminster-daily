## Lord's Day 43

### The Ninth Commandment

*Christian Gratitude*

#### Question 112

What is required in the ninth commandment?

##### Answer

That I bear false witness against no man, [^fna] nor falsify any man's words; [^fnb] that I be no backbiter, nor slanderer; [^fnc] that I do not judge, nor join in condemning any man rashly, or unheard; [^fnd] but that I avoid all sorts of lies and deceit, as the proper works of the devil, [^fne] unless I would bring down upon me the heavy wrath of God; [^fnf] likewise, that in judgment and all other dealings I love the truth, speak it uprightly and confess it; [^fng] also that I defend and promote, as much as I am able, the honor and good character of my neighbour. [^fnh]
