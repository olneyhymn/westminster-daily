## Lord's Day 52

### The Sixth Petition and the Conclusion

*Christian Gratitude*

#### Question 127

Which is the sixth petition?

##### Answer

"And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil"; that is, since we are so weak in ourselves, that we cannot stand a moment; [^fn127a] and besides this, since our mortal enemies, the devil, [^fn127b] the world, [^fn127c] and our own flesh, [^fn127d] cease not to assault us, do thou therefore preserve and strengthen us by the power of thy Holy Spirit, that we may not be overcome in this spiritual warfare, [^fn127e] but constantly and strenuously may resist our foes, till at last we obtain a complete victory. [^fn127f]

#### Question 128

How dost thou conclude thy prayer?

##### Answer

"For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, forever"; that is, all these we ask of thee, because thou, being our King and almighty, art willing and able to give us all good; [^fn128a] and all this we pray for, that thereby not we, but thy holy name, may be glorified for ever. [^fn128b]

#### Question 129

What does the word "Amen" signify?

##### Answer

"Amen" signifies, it shall truly and certainly be: for my prayer is more assuredly heard of God, than I feel in my heart that I desire these things of him. (a)

								2 Timothy 2:13 If we believe not, yet he abideth faithful: he cannot deny himself.
