#### Shorter Catechism

<span class="q">Q 101.</span> What do we pray for in the first petition?

<span class="q">A.</span> In the first petition, which is, Hallowed be thy name, we pray that God would enable us, and others, to glorify him in all that whereby he maketh himself known;[^fnref:wsc1] and that he would dispose all things to his own glory.[^fnref:wsc2]

#### Larger Catechism

<span class="q">Q 190.</span> What do we pray for in the first petition?

<span class="q">A.</span> In the first petition, (which is, Hallowed be thy name,[^fnref:wlc1]) acknowledging the utter inability and indisposition that is in ourselves and all men to honor God aright,[^fnref:wlc2] we pray, that God would by his grace enable and incline us and others to know, to acknowledge, and highly to esteem him,[^fnref:wlc3] his titles,[^fnref:wlc4] attributes,[^fnref:wlc5] ordinances, Word,[^fnref:wlc6] works, and whatsoever he is pleased to make himself known by;[^fnref:wlc7] and to glorify him in thought, word,[^fnref:wlc8] and deed:[^fnref:wlc9] that he would prevent and remove atheism,[^fnref:wlc10] ignorance,[^fnref:wlc11] idolatry,[^fnref:wlc12] profaneness,[^fnref:wlc13] and whatsoever is dishonorable to him;[^fnref:wlc14] and, by his overruling providence, direct and dispose of all things to his own glory.[^fnref:wlc15]
