#### Shorter Catechism

26\. How doth Christ execute the office of a king?

A. Christ executeth the office of a king, in subduing us to himself, in ruling and defending us,[^fnref:wsc1] and in restraining and conquering all his and our enemies.[^fnref:wsc2]

#### Larger Catechism

45\. How doth Christ execute the office of a king?

A. Christ executeth the office of a king, in calling out of the world a people to himself,[^fnref:wlc1] and giving them officers,[^fnref:wlc2] laws,[^fnref:wlc3] and censures, by which he visibly governs them;[^fnref:wlc4] in bestowing saving grace upon his elect,[^fnref:wlc5] rewarding their obedience,[^fnref:wlc6] and correcting them for their sins,[^fnref:wlc7] preserving and supporting them under all their temptations and sufferings,[^fnref:wlc8] restraining and overcoming all their enemies,[^fnref:wlc9] and powerfully ordering all things for his own glory,[^fnref:wlc10] and their good;[^fnref:wlc11] and also in taking vengeance on the rest, who know not God, and obey not the gospel.[^fnref:wlc12]
