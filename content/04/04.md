#### Shorter Catechism

27. Wherein did Christ's humiliation consist?

A. Christ's humiliation consisted in his being born, and that in a low condition,[^fnref:wsc1] made under the law,[^fnref:wsc2] undergoing the miseries of this life,[^fnref:wsc3] the wrath of God,[^fnref:wsc4] and the cursed death of the cross;[^fnref:wsc5] in being buried, and continuing under the power of death for a time.[^fnref:wsc6]

#### Larger Catechism

46. What was the estate of Christ's humiliation?

A. The estate of Christ's humiliation was that low condition, wherein he for our sakes, emptying himself of his glory, took upon him the form of a servant,[^fnref:wlc1] in his conception[^fnref:wlc2] and birth,[^fnref:wlc3] life,[^fnref:wlc4] death,[^fnref:wlc5] and after his death,[^fnref:wlc6] until his resurrection.[^fnref:wlc7]

And being found in human form, he humbled himself by becoming obedient to the point of death, even death on a cross.  (<a href="http://www.esv.org" class="copyright">ESV</a>)</p> </div> </div>
