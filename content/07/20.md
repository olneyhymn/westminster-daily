#### Larger Catechism

112\. What is required in the third commandment?

A. The third commandment requires, that the name of God, his titles, attributes,[^fnref:wlc1] ordinances,[^fnref:wlc2] the Word,[^fnref:wlc3] sacraments,[^fnref:wlc4] prayer,[^fnref:wlc5] oaths,[^fnref:wlc6] vows,[^fnref:wlc7] lots,[^fnref:wlc8] his works,[^fnref:wlc9] and whatsoever else there is whereby he makes himself known, be holily and reverently used in thought,[^fnref:wlc10] meditation,[^fnref:wlc11] word,[^fnref:wlc12] and writing;[^fnref:wlc13] by an holy profession,[^fnref:wlc14] and answerable conversation,[^fnref:wlc15] to the glory of God,[^fnref:wlc16] and the good of ourselves,[^fnref:wlc17] and others.[^fnref:wlc18]
