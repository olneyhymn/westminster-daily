#### Shorter Catechism

<span class="q">52.</span> What are the reasons annexed to the second commandment?

<span class="q">A.</span> The reasons annexed to the second commandment are, God's sovereignty over us,[^fnref:wsc1] his propriety in us,[^fnref:wsc2] and the zeal he hath to his own worship.[^fnref:wsc3]

#### Larger Catechism

<span class="q">110.</span> What are the reasons annexed to the second commandment, the more to enforce it?

<span class="q">A.</span> The reasons annexed to the second commandment, the more to enforce it, contained in these words, For I the LORD thy God am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third and fourth generation of them that hate me; and shewing mercy unto thousands of them that love me, and keep my commandments;[^fnref:wlc1] are, besides God's sovereignty over us, and propriety in us,[^fnref:wlc2] his fervent zeal for his own worship,[^fnref:wlc3] and his revengeful indignation against all false worship, as being a spiritual whoredom;[^fnref:wlc4] accounting the breakers of this commandment such as hate him, and threatening to punish them unto divers generations;[^fnref:wlc5] and esteeming the observers of it such as love him and keep his commandments, and promising mercy to them unto many generations.[^fnref:wlc6]
