#### Shorter Catechism

<span class="q">46.</span> What is required in the first commandment?

<span class="q">A.</span> The first commandment requireth us to know and acknowledge God to be the only true God, and our God; and to worship and glorify him accordingly.[^fnref:wsc1]

#### Larger Catechism

<span class="q">104.</span> What are the duties required in the first commandment?

<span class="q">A.</span> The duties required in the first commandment are, the knowing and acknowledging of God to be the only true God, and our God;[^fnref:wlc1] and to worship and glorify him accordingly,[^fnref:wlc2] by thinking,[^fnref:wlc3] meditating,[^fnref:wlc4] remembering,[^fnref:wlc5] highly esteeming,[^fnref:wlc6] honoring,[^fnref:wlc7] adoring,[^fnref:wlc8] choosing,[^fnref:wlc9] loving,[^fnref:wlc10] desiring,[^fnref:wlc11] fearing of him;[^fnref:wlc12] believing him;[^fnref:wlc13] trusting,[^fnref:wlc14] hoping,[^fnref:wlc15] delighting,[^fnref:wlc16] rejoicing in him;[^fnref:wlc17] being zealous for him;[^fnref:wlc18] calling upon him, giving all praise and thanks,[^fnref:wlc19] and yielding all obedience and submission to him with the whole man;[^fnref:wlc20] being careful in all things to please him,[^fnref:wlc21] and sorrowful when in anything he is offended;[^fnref:wlc22] and walking humbly with him.[^fnref:wlc23]
